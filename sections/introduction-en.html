Introduction back to ToC

Occupants behaviour in a building environment deals with a range of different kinds of entities: building objects, users characteristics, activities, occupancy, environmental conditions, user’s settings, and so on. IEA – EBC Annex 66 and 79 clearly set the model parameters that may be considered towards the definition of Occupant Behaviour in Buildings.

The key parameters (occupants’ drivers, needs, actions, states) are incorporating in the occupants behaviour ontology as presented in the following sections.
